# Unified Customer Intelligence System

## Integrated Modules
1. KPI Monitoring
2. Customer Retention Analytics
3. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
4. Revenue Forecasting
5. Predictive Risk Scoring

## Key Business Metrics
- Total Customers: 500

- Retention Rate: 51.0%
- Customer Lifetime Value: 6098.57
- Forecast Revenue: 266812.65
- High Risk Customers: 245

## Executive Insights
- Revenue is expected to grow to 266,812.65.
- More than half of customers are retained.
- 245 customers have high churn risk and require attention.
- Customer value remains stable with a CLV of 6098.57.

## Business Tradeoffs
- Forecasting improves planning but depends on assumptions.
- Risk prediction improves retention but may generate false positives.
- KPI monitoring provides visibility but requires continuous updates.

## Conclusion
A unified business intelligence platform enables organizations to combine forecasting, retention analytics, KPI monitoring, and risk prediction into one decision-support system.
