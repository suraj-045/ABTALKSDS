Business Interpretation Report

Purpose

Explain why the model predicts customer churn risk.

Feature Importance Ranking

1. Amount
2. Time
3. Segment

Interpretation

Amount

The strongest predictor of customer behavior.

Customers with lower transaction amounts are more likely to be classified as high-risk customers.

Time

Transaction timing provides additional behavioral information and helps improve prediction accuracy.

Segment

Customer segmentation adds context about behavioral patterns and customer groups.

Business Impact

These insights help organizations:

* Understand customer behavior
* Improve retention strategies
* Focus on high-risk customers
* Increase trust in AI-driven decisions

Conclusion

Customer spending behavior is the most important driver behind churn prediction decisions in the Customer Intelligence Platform.
