# Customer Risk Prediction Report

## Objective
Identify high-risk customers likely to churn.

## Approach
- Used customer behavior (Amount, Time)
- Applied segmentation (K-Means)
- Built logistic regression model

## Results
- Customers ranked by churn probability
- Top 10 high-risk customers identified

## Business Strategy
- Offer discounts to high-risk customers
- Improve engagement and support
- Provide personalized recommendations

## Conclusion
Predictive analytics helps businesses proactively reduce churn and improve customer retention.
