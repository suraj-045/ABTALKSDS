# Customer Persona Report

## Persona 1: Regular Customers

Characteristics:
- Higher average activity time
- Consistent customer behavior
- Moderate transaction amounts

Business Strategy:
- Send periodic promotional offers
- Encourage repeat engagement
- Provide personalized recommendations

---

## Persona 2: Premium Customers

Characteristics:
- Lower average activity time
- Active purchasing behavior
- Valuable customer segment

Business Strategy:
- Offer loyalty rewards
- Provide exclusive discounts
- Create premium membership benefits

## Conclusion

Customer segmentation helps identify different customer groups and enables businesses to design targeted marketing strategies.
### Strategy
- Loyalty rewards
- Premium offers
- Personalized experiences
