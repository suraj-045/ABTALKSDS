Day 47 - Capstone Project Proposal

Project Title

Customer Intelligence Platform

Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to understand customer behavior, predict churn, identify high-value customers, and make data-driven decisions. A centralized Customer Intelligence Platform can help organizations improve retention, increase revenue, and enhance customer experience.

Business Objectives

* Predict customer churn risk
* Segment customers into meaningful groups
* Forecast future revenue and customer growth
* Monitor business KPIs
* Detect unusual customer behavior
* Provide executive-level dashboards

Dataset

Primary Dataset:

* Credit Card Transactions Dataset

Features Used:

* Amount
* Time
* Transaction Patterns
* Customer Segments
* Churn Indicators

Key Modules

1. Customer Segmentation

Group customers using K-Means clustering.

2. Recommendation Engine

Suggest similar customers or products.

3. Anomaly Detection

Identify suspicious customer behavior.

4. Churn Prediction

Predict high-risk customers.

5. Revenue Forecasting

Estimate future business performance.

6. KPI Dashboard

Monitor customer and revenue metrics.

7. API Deployment

Serve predictions through APIs.

8. Monitoring System

Track prediction requests and reliability.

Expected Outcome

A complete Customer Intelligence Platform capable of supporting business decision-making through analytics, prediction, forecasting, and visualization.
