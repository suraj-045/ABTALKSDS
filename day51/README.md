Day 51 - Explainable AI

Objective

Understand why the Customer Intelligence Platform makes customer churn predictions.

Techniques Used

* Random Forest Classifier
* Feature Importance Analysis
* Explainable AI (XAI)

Features Analyzed

* Amount
* Time
* Segment

Explainability Workflow

Customer Data
↓
Feature Engineering
↓
Random Forest Model
↓
Feature Importance Analysis
↓
Business Interpretation

Key Findings

* Amount was the most influential feature.
* Time provided additional customer behavior insights.
* Segment contributed to customer grouping and prediction quality.

Business Benefits

* Increased transparency
* Better stakeholder trust
* Easier interpretation of predictions
* Improved business decision-making

Outcome

Successfully analyzed and explained the main factors driving customer churn predictions within the Customer Intelligence Platform.
